About Us

The Event Planning and Execution Committee (EPEC) is the heart of all the vibrant events and fests conducted at our college every academic year. From spirited competitions to grand cultural nights, EPEC ensures every event is a resounding success.

Success Stories

Priya Sharma Batch of 2024 | EPEC Core Member

Priya joined EPEC in her first year at VIT Pune, eager to learn and contribute. She quickly became known for her creativity and teamwork. In her third year, Priya led the organization of the annual EPEC Tech Fest, managing a team of 40+ students and collaborating with industry experts. "EPEC gave me the confidence to lead and innovate. Organizing the Tech Fest was a turning point in my journey, and the skills I gained helped me secure an internship at a top tech company." Today, Priya is a proud EPEC alumna, inspiring juniors and giving back to the club through mentorship sessions.
